Decane Forex Investment Limited

Decane Forex Investment Limited is a private enterprise registered in Nigeria as a limited liability company to offer financial support and education to individuals, both in Nigeria and beyond shores.

Our purpose is to create a platform where all individuals can be financially stable, in order to improve lives through financial education and financial investment.

We aim to raise millions of investors around the world, give mentorship to young entrepreneurs and make them grow in order to achieve financial confidence and freedom.

DecaneFx Investment is a brand that has been operating since 2018 till date.

DecaneFx helps to raise investors, not by investing with the company alone but also by giving them guidance on how they can establish and manage their own businesses

Our Vision

To create a society where every individual is capable of affording their needs and wants without the need to obtain a loan.

Our Mission

To bring to all humanity financial opportunities and education to make them financially stable.
